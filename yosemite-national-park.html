Yosemite National Park

Set back in central California far to the east of San Francisco, Yosemite National Park is a favorite destination of adventurers of all kinds. With massive granite peaks and tree-filled valleys, this park offers fun of all varieties. Flowing waterfalls serve to accent the majesty of this park, especially in the winter and spring.
