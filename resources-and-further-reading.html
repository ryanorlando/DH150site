Resources and Further Reading

Below are all of the informational pages and news articles used to research this project. All of these resources present great further reading and are sure to provide even more information than I was able to present in this project.

In addition, for a research study on the need to diversify the National Park System, here is a link to a study conducted by researchers at Colorado State University and published by the National Park Service.

Finally, I want to highlight the National Park Foundation. The NPF is a foundation serving a number of functions, with the goal of improving education and "creating equal and abundant opportunities for all audiences" to experience our National Parks.
